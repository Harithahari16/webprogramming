EMMA

Novel by Jane Austen

Emma,by jane austen ,is a novel about youthful hubris and romantic misunderstandings.

It is set in the fictional country village of highbury and the surrounding estates of hartfield,

Randalls and Donwell Abbey,and involves the relationships among people from a small number of families.

Characters:Emma,George,Jane,Frank,Harriet,Mr.Elton,more.
